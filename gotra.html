Know Your Gotra

A gotra, a lineage segment within an Indian caste that forbids intermarriage based on the pedigree of members from a common mythical ancestor. The practice of forbidding marriage between members of the same Gotra was intended to free the Gotra from genetic contamination and to expand the influence of a particular Gotra through wider alliances with other powerful bloodlines.
